# Practical GRC Risk Identification Workshop

Hands-on lesson materials, templates, case study, and facilitator guide for **practical Risk Identification** using the Cause → Event → Impact method.

Designed for live GRC streaming sessions and real organizational workshops.

## What's Included

- **LESSON_PLAN.md** – Detailed 2.5–3 hour timed agenda with facilitation notes
- **CASE_STUDY.md** – Apex Financial Services (Mobile Banking + Cloud Migration)
- **templates/**
  - `risk_register_template.csv` – Blank professional template
  - `sample_populated_risk_register.csv` – Fully worked examples
  - `risk_identification_inputs_checklist.md` – What real organizations actually gather
- **FACILITATOR_GUIDE.md** – Screen-share tips, chat prompts, OBS notes

## How to Use

1. Clone or download this repository.
2. Open the Risk Register CSV in Google Sheets or Excel.
3. Follow the Lesson Plan for a live walkthrough or internal workshop.
4. Use the Case Study as the scenario.

## Educational Use

These materials are provided for educational and professional development purposes. They represent general best practices and are not a substitute for organization-specific risk management advice or legal/regulatory counsel.
