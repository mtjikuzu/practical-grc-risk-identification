# Case Study: Apex Financial Services – Mobile Banking App Launch & Cloud Migration

## Organization Profile
Apex Financial Services is a mid-sized regional financial services organization with approximately 850 employees. It serves retail customers and SMEs with traditional banking products and is now aggressively expanding its digital offerings. The organization is regulated and is required to protect customer financial data, maintain high availability of critical services, and demonstrate effective risk management to regulators and external auditors.

**Current state:** Most core systems and customer data remain on-premise. Some SaaS applications are already in use.

## The Strategic Initiative
The Board has approved a high-priority digital transformation project with a 4-month timeline to:

1. Design, develop, and launch a new native mobile banking application (iOS and Android) featuring account management, transfers, bill payments, biometric authentication, and push notifications.
2. Migrate selected customer-facing services, authentication components, and customer data stores to a major public cloud provider.
3. Decommission corresponding on-premise infrastructure after successful cutover and stabilization.

## Business Drivers
- Competitive pressure from digital-native banks and fintechs
- Strong customer demand for a modern mobile experience
- Expected cost savings and improved scalability
- Explicit Board-level commitment to digital strategy

## Constraints and Realities
- Aggressive timeline driven by a fixed marketing launch date
- Limited internal expertise in cloud security architecture and operations
- Existing vulnerability management and change management processes are relatively basic
- Multiple third parties involved (cloud provider, mobile development partner, possibly managed security services)
- Full regulatory compliance must be maintained throughout the transition

## Available Inputs for the Risk Identification Workshop
The following materials are available for the facilitated risk identification exercise:
- High-level project plan and architecture overview
- Partial asset inventory
- Recent internal audit findings related to change management
- Sample vulnerability scan results from the current environment
- Summary of key regulatory requirements concerning customer data protection and operational resilience
- Incident and near-miss log for the previous 18 months

This scenario is designed to surface cyber, operational, third-party, compliance, and change-management risks in a realistic and interconnected way.
